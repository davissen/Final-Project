How to play the Space Invaderz game

In order to build the game a local server environment software was required by the phaser. For windows users, WAMP Server and XAMPP were recommended. For OS X users, MAMP was recommeded.

Use the Left and Right arrow to direct your spaceship.

Avoid the green bullets fired by the enemies, if your ship is hit, you'll lose a life (shown encircle in red above)

Use the spacebar to attack. 10 points are awarded per enemies killed and 1000 points for completing a level.

The user can access a menu during the game, where he can restart the game.
